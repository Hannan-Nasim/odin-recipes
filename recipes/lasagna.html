Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a hearty Italian classic made with layers of tender pasta sheets, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. Baked to golden perfection, it’s a warm, comforting dish that brings together bold flavors and satisfying textures. Whether served as a main course or for a special occasion, lasagna offers a deliciously layered experience in every bite. Its mix of savory sauces, gooey cheese, and pasta makes it a timeless favorite for both casual dinners and festive gatherings.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
