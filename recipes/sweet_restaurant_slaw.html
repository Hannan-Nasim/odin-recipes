Sweet Restaurant Slaw

Description

Sweet restaurant slaw is a crisp, refreshing side dish made with shredded cabbage, carrots, and a creamy, tangy-sweet dressing. It offers a perfect balance of textures and flavors, making it a favorite companion to rich or spicy mains. This slaw stands out with its smooth blend of sugar, vinegar, and a touch of mayonnaise, creating a light yet indulgent flavor. It's a go-to side that adds crunch and contrast to everything from barbecue to fried chicken.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) bag coleslaw mix
  • 2 tablespoons diced onion
  • ⅔ cup creamy salad dressing (such as Miracle Whip)
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon white vinegar
  • ½ teaspoon poppy seeds
  • ¼ teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Combine coleslaw mix and onion in a large bowl.
  3. Whisk salad dressing, sugar, vegetable oil, vinegar, poppy seeds, and salt together in a medium bowl until blended.
  4. Pour dressing over coleslaw mixture and toss to coat. Chill for at least 2 hours before serving.Gather the ingredients.
